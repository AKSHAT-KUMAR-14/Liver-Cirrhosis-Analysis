Services

  • Cardiology

    Our cardiology department provides a range of diagnostic and interventional procedures to help diagnose and treat heart conditions.

  • Dental Care

    We offer a comprehensive range of dental care services, from routine check-ups and cleanings to more complex treatments.

  • Emergency Room

    Our emergency room is open 24/7 to provide prompt, compassionate care for patients with life-threatening or non-life-threatening emergencies.

  • Gastroenterology

    Our gastroenterology department offers a variety of diagnostic and treatment services for disorders of the stomach, small intestine, large intestine, liver, and gallbladder.

  • Neurology

    The neurology department provides comprehensive diagnostic and interventional care for patients with disorders of the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nervous system.

  • Orthopedics

    The orthopedics department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders involving the musculoskeletal system.

  • Outpatient Surgery

    We offer a variety of outpatient surgical procedures to treat a range of conditions.

  • Radiology

    The radiology department offers a comprehensive range of diagnostic imaging services to assist in the diagnosis and treatment of medical conditions.
